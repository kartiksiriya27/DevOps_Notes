1] Cache 
• A storage space for files like images and videos
• Helps pages load faster by saving parts of pages
• Reduces stress on the server
• Stored in high-speed hardware components like RAM

2] Cookies 
• Small text files that store information about users
• Helps websites identify users and track their browsing activity
• Can store information like login credentials, preferences, and shopping cart items
• Can be used to remember users across different sites
